## What you’ll find here

- **Unified catalog.** Standardized, searchable info across mice, keyboards, monitors, mousepads, and GPUs. Coverage expands into headsets and chairs next.  
- **Hubs and comparison tools.** Filter by what matters, build shortlists, and compare side by side.  
- **Reviews built for decisions.** Clear summaries, who it’s for, key takeaways, pros and cons, and sensible alternatives.  
- **Guides that curate.** Best picks by budget and playstyle, with setup tips for gaming, work, and creation.  
- **News you can use.** Concise launch coverage tied back into the catalog for deeper research.

## Our mission and vision

**Mission:** make choosing PC gear feel simple and empowering. We write in plain language, keep pages fast, and focus on the outcomes that matter to you.

**Vision:** a single trustworthy path from question to confident pick. Reviews, guides, and hubs live on one connected catalog with clear structure, consistent labeling, and side-by-side comparisons. We keep pages fresh as products evolve and prices change.

## How we work {#how-we-work}

**In short:** we verify, we explain, and we keep things current.

1. **Verify at the source.** We confirm key details using official manufacturer materials and note check dates.  
2. **Normalize and organize.** Consistent names and units make comparisons easy across categories.  
3. **Write for decisions.** Short paragraphs, scannable sections, and “why it matters” framing.  
4. **Be transparent.** Where helpful, we add callouts like “Verified Specs” and “Price & Availability” with dates.  
5. **Update with intent.** Guides and hubs are revisited as models change or pricing shifts.

## Authenticity cues you’ll see

- **Verified Specs** badge with a date  
- **Our Take** summary written in plain English  
- **Why It Made the List** bullets that focus on outcomes  
- **Price & Availability** with check dates  
- **Original visuals** or official media with descriptive alt text

## Our values {#our-values}

- **User first.** Clarity, confidence, and your time come first.

- **Accuracy.** We verify claims and correct mistakes promptly.  
- **Integrity and independence.** Picks are merit-based.

- **Performance and UX.** Fast pages and sensible ad density.
