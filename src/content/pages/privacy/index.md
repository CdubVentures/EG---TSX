# Privacy Policy

Effective date: **November 5, 2025**

EG Gear is owned and operated by **Nalster LLC** (a California limited liability company). This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Summary

- We collect your **email and password** to create and secure your account and to run the comments system.  
- We log **IP address and basic device info** to protect the site (security, spam, and abuse prevention).  
- We **do not use cookies** or similar trackers on our site today.  
- We **do not send marketing emails**, **do not run targeted advertising**, and **do not sell or “share”** personal information for cross-context behavioral ads.  
- We use **Amazon affiliate links**. When you click one, Amazon may use its own identifiers; we disclose: **“As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.”**  
- If you delete your account, your past comments remain but are **anonymized** (author name removed); we retain minimal logs for site security.  
- You can request to **access, correct, or delete** your data at any time via **[/contact](/contact)**.  
- Our site is **for ages 13+**; we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

---

## What we collect

**Account & profile**  
Email, username, and password (stored as a salted, one-way hash). Optional profile fields you add (e.g., display name or avatar).

**Content you submit**  
Comments and messages you post or send to us (public comments are visible to others).

**Technical & security data**  
IP address, timestamps, user-agent, referrer, and pages requested; used for security, spam/abuse prevention, and to operate the service.

**Affiliate click context**  
When you click a product link that goes to a retailer (e.g., Amazon), we record that an outbound click occurred. The retailer’s site handles any further data under its own policy. Required disclosure: **As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.**

We do **not** collect sensitive personal information.

## Cookies and similar technologies

We **do not set cookies** or similar identifiers on your device and we do not use pixels or cross-site trackers on our pages. Because we don’t set non-essential cookies, you won’t see a cookie banner here. If you follow an **Amazon** link, that retailer may use its own cookies/identifiers under its policies.

## How we use information

- **Provide and secure the service:** account login, comments, spam/abuse prevention, troubleshooting.  
- **Operate retailer links:** attribute outbound clicks and measure aggregate performance (no personalized ad profiles).  
- **Communicate with you:** account-related messages and responses to requests (no marketing newsletters).

## What we do not do

- No targeted or personalized advertising.  
- No marketing emails.  
- No sale or “sharing” of personal information as defined by California law.

## When we share information

We do **not** sell personal information. We share limited data with:

- **Service providers** that help run the site (e.g., hosting/security/email); they act under contract and may only use data to provide services to us.  
- **Retailers** you choose to visit by clicking our product links (e.g., Amazon); your use of those sites is governed by their policies.  
- **Legal needs** when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of our service.

## Affiliate disclosure

EG Gear participates in the **Amazon Associates Program**. **As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.** This disclosure appears site-wide and near relevant links.

## Data retention & deletion

- **Accounts:** kept while your account is active. If you delete your account, we **anonymize** authorship on past comments (e.g., display “Anonymous”) and retain minimal records necessary for security, troubleshooting, or legal obligations.  
- **Security logs:** retained for a limited period necessary to detect, investigate, and prevent abuse.  
- **Backups:** retained for continuity and securely cycled.

## Your rights

**All users:** request **access**, **correction**, or **deletion** of your personal data via **[/contact](/contact)**.

**California and certain U.S. states:** you may have rights to access, delete, correct, and to opt out of **sale** or **sharing** of personal information. We do **not** sell or share personal information, but we honor applicable rights requests.

**EU/UK visitors (GDPR):** you may have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Our legal bases are **contract** (to provide your account/comments) and **legitimate interests** (security and site reliability).

We will verify requests and respond as required by law.

## Children’s privacy

Our site is intended for audiences **13+**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can remove it.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account data and site operations. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## International transfers

We may process data in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards.

## Changes to this policy

If our practices change, we will update this page and revise the date at the top. For significant changes, we may provide an on-site notice.

## Contact

Questions or requests? Visit our **[Contact page](/contact)**.
